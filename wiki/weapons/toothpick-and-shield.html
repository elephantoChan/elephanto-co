Toothpick & Shield

Tank the damage! And keep your teeth clean...!

  • Unlock Level - 12
  • Unlock Place - Defeating Overlord
  • Special - Receive 25% less damage.

Main attack

Poke your Pick to deal area damage in front of you at close range.

  • Damage - 20
  • Hit speed - 0.4s
  • First hit - 0.075s
  • Range - 2.7m

Combo attack

After you receive 15 hits from enemies, your next attack is an area slam that deals damage in a large area around you and Stuns non-boss monsters.

  • Charge time - 20 hits
  • Damage - 100
  • Range - 3.5m
  • Spread - 360°
