Medicine Ball

The secret to good health!

Main attack

Swing your Ball to deal area damage in front of you at close range.

  • Damage - 40
  • Hit speed - 0.7s
  • First hit - 0.25s
  • Range - 2.7m
  • Spread - 50°

Combo attack

Every 3rd attack also heals you and all nearby friendlies in a big area all around you.

  • Charge time - 3 attacks
  • Damage - 80
  • Healing - 120
  • Range - 2.7m
  • Radius - 4m
  • Spread - 50°
